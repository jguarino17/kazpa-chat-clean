What Is A Prop Firm?


First off, let's clarify what a prop firm is. A proprietary, or 'prop', firm provides traders with capital to trade and shares in the profits generated. It's a fantastic way for skilled traders to access larger sums of money without risking their own capital.

Choosing The Right Prop Firm


When selecting a prop firm, especially for algorithmic trading with The Wraith Bot, consider these key factors:


Account Size: Opt for accounts of $100,000 or higher to fully leverage the capabilities of algorithms.


Drawdown Limits: Understand the daily drawdown and total loss limits to manage risk effectively.


Withdrawal Times: Know when and how you can withdraw your profits.


Profit Splits: Evaluate how profits are shared between you and the firm.


Rules and Guidelines: Thoroughly review all rules and guidelines or contact the firm's support for clarifications.
