kazpa FAQs — Frequently Asked Questions

What is kazpa?
kazpa is a professional trading software company. We license algorithmic trading software that runs inside MetaTrader 5 (MT5) on your own broker account. We do not manage funds, provide financial advice, or guarantee outcomes.

What software does kazpa offer?
kazpa licenses the Vista Suite, which includes two distinct trading softwares:
- VistaONE — runs on EURUSD, M15 timeframe. Designed for structured, lower-stress, longer-term automation.
- VistaX — runs on XAUUSD (Gold), M5 timeframe. Designed for experienced users who want session-based execution with higher risk and higher responsibility.

Is kazpa passive income?
No. kazpa software is automated, but it is not passive. You are responsible for monitoring execution, managing risk, controlling AutoTrading, and pausing during unfavorable conditions. Automation reduces emotional trading — it does not remove responsibility.

Can I lose money?
Yes. Trading involves risk. Losses and drawdown are possible. No outcome is guaranteed. Only use capital you can afford to lose. Demo testing is strongly encouraged before any live use.

What platform does kazpa run on?
MetaTrader 5 (MT5) only. MT4 is not supported.

Do I need a VPS?
A VPS is strongly recommended. It keeps MT5 running 24/7 without needing your personal computer on. You access your VPS using the Microsoft App, available on Windows, Mac, iPhone, and Android.

How do I get started?
The correct setup order is:
1. Create and verify a broker account with MT5 support
2. Set up a VPS
3. Install MT5 on your VPS
4. Submit the License Activation Form in your member dashboard
5. kazpa generates and sends your license key (within 24 hours)
6. Install the software, configure risk settings, test on demo

Where do I find my license key?
In your member dashboard at kazpa.io/dashboard under "Trading Accounts". Your license key is shown for each active account.

Can I use kazpa with any broker?
You are responsible for choosing your own broker. kazpa does not recommend or endorse specific brokers. Your broker must support MetaTrader 5 (MT5), offer adequate leverage, and be available in your region. kazpa has no broker affiliations.

How do I know the software is working?
When the software is correctly attached and running in MT5, a dark BLUE HAT ICON appears in the top-right corner of the chart. If the hat is not dark blue or is missing, the software was not installed correctly.

What do I do if the software is not trading?
Work through these checks in order:
1. Confirm AutoTrading is ON (green button in MT5)
2. Confirm the blue hat icon is showing on the correct chart
3. Confirm you are on the correct symbol and timeframe (EURUSD M15 for VistaONE, XAUUSD M5 for VistaX)
4. Confirm the market is open
5. Check the Experts and Journal tabs in MT5 for error messages
6. Contact support if the issue persists
