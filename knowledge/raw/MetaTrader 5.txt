MetaTrader 5 
Desktop App Setup Guide
________________


MetaTrader is a popular electronic trading platform primarily designed for retail forex trading, though it also supports other asset classes like stocks and commodities. It equips traders with a comprehensive toolkit for analyzing financial markets, executing trades, and managing positions. Thanks to its user-friendly interface and extensive features, MetaTrader is the preferred choice of both beginner and experienced traders around the world.
In order to trade in the Forex market, you will need to download and setup MetaTrader 4 or 5. This is where you will load your kazpa algorithms to trade for you.
Versions
MetaTrader offers two main versions:
* MetaTrader 4 (MT4)
* MetaTrader 5 (MT5)
Each version comes with unique features and capabilities, providing traders access to a wide range of technical indicators, charting tools, and automated trading systems, known as Expert Advisors (EAs), to enhance their trading strategies.
You will download the version based off the kazpa algorithm you are running

Download MetaTrader 4 or 5


First, log into your VPS. That is where you will download MetaTrader. You will need to download the MetaTrader 4 or 5  desktop application from your broker to begin trading. (depending on which algorithm you are using, you will download Metatrader 4 or 5. If you are unsure, contact your kazpa Support Manager or the Customer Support Team at support@kazpa.ai) 


1. Download the MT4/5 Platform
* Log into your VPS 
* Visit your broker’s website to download the MT4/5 platform.
* Look for the "Download" section, usually found on the homepage or under the "Trading Platforms" tab.
* Download the MT4/5 setup file (often named mt4setup.exe or something similar).


2. Run the MT4/5 Installer
* After downloading, locate the setup file in your computer's "Downloads" folder or wherever you saved it
* Double-click the .exe file to launch the installation process. You may be prompted by a User Account Control (UAC) window asking for permission to make changes to your computer. Click Yes to proceed.


3. Follow the Installation Wizard
* The MT4/5 Setup Wizard will open. Review the End User License Agreement (EULA) and click Next or Agree to accept the terms and continue the installation.
* The installer will ask where you want to install MT4/5. The default directory is usually fine, but you can choose a custom location if preferred.
* Click Next to begin the installation.


4. Complete the Installation
* The installer will begin copying files to your system, which should only take a few minutes.
* Once the process is finished, you will be prompted to launch MT4/5 immediately or exit the installer. Make sure the option to
* "Launch MetaTrader 4/5" is checked, and click Finish.


5. Open MT4/5 and Log In
* When MT4/5 opens for the first time, you will be prompted to either: 
   * Open a New Demo Account (for practice trading)
   * Log In to an Existing Account (if you have a live or demo account with your broker).
* Select the appropriate option and enter the login credentials provided by your broker (account number, password, and server).


Verify Account Login
To confirm your login:
* In the Navigator section, ensure that your account is displayed.
* In the Terminal section, select the Trade tab to view your balance.
