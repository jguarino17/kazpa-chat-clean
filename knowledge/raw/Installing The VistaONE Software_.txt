Installing The VistaONE Software:


Step 1: Copy the software to VPS
1. Connect to your VPS via Remote Desktop.
2. Locate the EA on your desktop (VistaONE.ex5 file).
3. Transfer the EA file to the VPS by copying it from your desktop and pasting it onto the VPS desktop.
Step 2: Load software into MetaTrader 5
1. Open MetaTrader 5 on your VPS.
2. Go to File > Open Data Folder.
3. Open the MQL5 > Experts > Advisors folder.
4. Paste the EA file into the Advisors folder.
5. Close and restart MetaTrader 5 for the EA to appear.
Step 3: Turn on Auto Trading
1. Ensure Auto Trading is enabled: Click the "Auto Trading" button in MetaTrader 5 (it should be green).
2. Enable Auto Trading in the software: Drag the EA from the Navigator panel to the desired chart (EURUSD.PRO), and in the EA settings, ensure “Allow Algo Trading” is checked.
Step 4: Load the Set Files
1. Apply Set Files: In the software settings panel, select the “Inputs” tab 
2. Click "Load" and select the set file provided for optimal settings. (Conservative, Normal, Aggressive)
3. Apply settings and confirm by clicking "OK".
Step 5: Start Trading
1. Verify the software is running: The blue hat in the top right of the chart indicates the software is working. If the hat is not dark blue, then the software was not installed correctly.
2. Monitor your trades: Keep track of your positions via the "Trade" tab at the bottom of MetaTrader 5.
Now, the software should be up and running on your MetaTrader 5, and you are set to start trading!
